ABOUT COLLEGE:

Ideal Institute of Engineering (IIE), established in 2010, is a private engineering college located in Kalyani, West Bengal. Affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) and approved by AICTE, the college offers undergraduate programs like B.Tech in CSE, EE, ME, CE, and ECE, as well as courses like BBA. The campus spans around 6 acres and includes modern classrooms, laboratories, a digital library, hostels, sports facilities, and Wi-Fi access. IIE has an active Training & Placement Cell that connects students with top recruiters such as TCS, Infosys, Wipro, and Capgemini, although placement rates and packages vary by branch. Students appreciate the supportive faculty and campus life, though some reviews mention issues with management and infrastructure maintenance. Overall, IIE is considered a good choice for students looking for an affordable and value-driven technical education in West Bengal.
